Possibly — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026  |  Effective date: March 29, 2026

Core principle: We do NOT store your conversation content on our servers.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your chat messages or conversation content
  • Your name, phone number, or contact information
  • Your location data
  • Financial information (payments processed by Apple/Google)
  • Contacts or address book data

Information We Collect

Anonymous Session Data

  • A temporary anonymous session ID (generated locally, not linked to your identity)
  • App usage patterns (e.g., number of analyses performed per session)

Analysis Metadata (not conversation content)

  • Aggregated analysis results (axis scores only: Maintain/Closer/Apart levels)
  • App performance data (response times, error rates)

All conversation text you upload is:

  1. Processed in memory only
  2. Used solely to generate your analysis
  3. Never stored on our servers
  4. Discarded immediately after analysis is complete

How We Use Information

  • To provide the relationship flow analysis service
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To prevent abuse and ensure safe use

  • App performance logs: Retained for 30 days, then deleted

Safe Mode & Crisis Detection

If our app detects language indicating potential crisis or self-harm, we display crisis support resources. We do not store or report these interactions.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Contact us with any questions.
